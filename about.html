About the Artist

Hi, I'm Willa

I specialize in hand-painted pet portraits using professional grade acrylics. I customize all my portraits to fit my clients' needs and aesthetic preferences.

During your initial consultation, we will discuss size, style, color schemes, etc. to make sure you are 100% satisified with the final product. My goal is to create a one of a kind portrait that puts your beloved pet's best paw forward.

As a fellow pet owner, I appreciate the distinct personality of every animal and strive to capture that individuality in each of my portraits.
