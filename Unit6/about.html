About the Artist

Hi, I'm Willa

I'm a native New Yorker with a passion for art and animals. Ever since I can remember, I have loved to paint and draw. I have also always loved animals, especially my 14-year-old miniature dachshund named Otto.



My Expertise

I specialize in portraits of dogs and cats in acrylics. I customize all of my paintings to fit my clients' needs and aesthetic preferences.

During your initial consultation, we will discuss size, style, color schemes, etc. to make sure you are 100% satisified with the final product. My goal is to create a one of a kind portrait that puts your beloved pet's best paw forward.

As a fellow pet owner, I appreciate the distinct personality of every animal and strive to capture that individuality in each of my pawtraits.
