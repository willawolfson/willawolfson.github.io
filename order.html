How to Order

The Basics

  • 1. Choose a high-resolution photo of your pet.
  • 2. Choose which canvas size you'd like (prices vary based on size).
  • 3. Submit your photo and size preference by emailing orders@pawtrait.nyc.
  • 4. Once I receive the photograph via email, we will discuss payment and shipping options.
